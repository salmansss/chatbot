Date Extended-SGS-2017-18
Last Date for filling the Application form of State Government Scholarship for Minority Communities-2017-18 is Extended up-to 20-02-2018

Facility available for checking Link Status of Aadhaar Card Number.
Student/ Institute can check- " Is Aadhaar Card No, is Link with Bank Account Number OR Not ? " You can check the link Status by dialing/calling on *99*99# OR on website-uidai.gov.in -> Aadhaar Services.If till date it is not link than contact to your bank for Linking of your Aadhaar Card No.with your Account on NPCI MAPPER. To ensure student has to check the link status again by using above available facility.

Helpline No.
Student can check the Status (Scrutinized by Inst./ Accepted / Rejected /etc.) of his/her filled application form of State Government Minority Scholarship by using there Application_ID. For Status ,instead of calling directly to DTE , Student are suggested to refer the website for smooth implementation of Scheme. If they have any query ,they can contact to Institute.

Provision of Undo Scruitiny Option in SGS.
Undo Scruitiny Option is provided in Institute Login. If Institute has done any mistake in a Scrutiny of State Government Scholarship application form than they can correct it by using Undo Scruitiny option.

Filling of Application Form-2016-17
Institute has to Inform to all Eligible Minority Candidate for Filling the Minority Scholarship Application Form-2016-17 without waiting up to the Last date. So that the process of scholarship disbursement can be started as early as possible.

Browser
It is to inform that to get faster access to the National Scholarship Portal (www.scholarships.gov.in) kindly use Mozilla Firefox Browser instead of Google Chrome. And for State Government Minority Scholarship (www.dtemaharashtra.gov.in/scholarships) use Google Chrome or Internet Explorer.

Merit cum Selection List-SGS-2015-16
Merit cum Selection List of State Government Scholarship-2015-16 is Generated.Candidate can Check Scholarship Status through their Application Id.

Aadhaar Number (UID)
Update option for Aadhaar number of candidate is given to Institute in their login.Institute has to Update the Aadhaar number of candidate and inform to the candidate for linking with their Bank account.

Use Browser
Use Internet Explorer

Minor-Major a/c in bank.
At the time of opening of the bank a/c,if the candidate is Minor(below 18 year) and now the candidate is Major(above 18 year),contact to bank for necessary changes if still a/c is minor in bank for E-transfer.

Website
For Merit cum Means based scholarship-www.scholarships.gov.in and for StateGovt.Scholarship- www.dtemaharashtra.gov.in/scholarships

LoginID(User Name) and Website for Institute.
LoginID(User Name) and Website for both the schemes i.e.Merit cum Means based scholarship(of GoI) and State Govt.scholarship (of Maharashtra Govt.) are different and for both schemes,User name(LoginId) and password are provided to that Institute whose name is in registered Institute List.

Renewal and Fresh scholarship.
The eligible candidate who has got one type of scholarship can apply as a RENEWAL for that type of scholarship only.Other candidate can apply as a FRESH scholarship.

Types of Scholarship schemes for Tech. and Prof.courses.
Both types of Scholarship scheme i.e.Merit cum Means based scholarship and State Govt.Scholarship scheme are different one.One who has got one type of scholarship can't apply for Renewal in other scholarship,they can apply as a RENEWAL in same type of Scholarship.If want to apply in other type of scholarship, they can apply as a FRESH scholarship.

Forgot password
INSTITUTE that comes under DTE can give a password to candidate who has forgot their own password through INSTITUTE LOGIN and on clicking retrive password in a institute pannel.

IFSC Code in Bank Details
IFSC Code of bank branch is compulsory for Transferring of Scholarship Amount

In case of Joint Account
First name should be the name of the scholarship holder in bank in case of Joint account.

Opening of Bank A/C
The name on account should be match with the name as in application form submitted by the candidate for scholarship.

Address of Regional Offices of DTE
For address click on Regional Offices. Link:http://www.dtemaharashtra.gov.in/ Scholarships/frmRegionalOffices.aspx
